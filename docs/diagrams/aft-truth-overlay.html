AFT — Attested Fact Trace

A living COGOBJ accumulates claims per attribute. Trust is not binary on the whole object — it is per field, scored by who asserted it, and only a Truth Root authority can rectify low-confidence claims. Caches then re-propagate like DNS.

AFT = is it true?
WHO = who authorized?
v0.1 · public demo
Locked rail split WHO proves authorization / named approver. AFT is a TRUTH judgment. A receipt can carry a signed AFT classification and make it un-fakeable — it cannot make the claim true. Different axes. Never fuse them into an oracle.
